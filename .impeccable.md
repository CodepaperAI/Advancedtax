## Design Context

### Users
Australian individuals, business owners, property investors, SMSF trustees, and growing SMEs comparing accounting, tax, advisory, and mortgage-aligned financial services. They arrive needing confidence, clarity, and a direct path to speak with a qualified adviser.

### Brand Personality
Established, precise, and human. The interface should feel premium and trustworthy without becoming cold or generic.

### Aesthetic Direction
Editorial finance and advisory: deep navy foundations, burnished gold details, expressive serif display typography, refined sans UI text, real team and office imagery, asymmetrical layouts, cinematic but purposeful Framer Motion animation, and strong conversion paths into consultation booking.

### Design Principles
- Lead with trust before explanation: show real people, locations, accreditations, and practical outcomes early.
- Avoid generic template rhythm: use varied editorial sections, image bands, atlas-style service grouping, timelines, and split compositions instead of repeating card grids.
- Keep motion purposeful: reveal hierarchy, guide scroll progress, support menus/forms/accordions, and respect reduced motion.
- Maintain polished responsiveness: no horizontal scroll, no overlap, stable image aspect ratios, readable mobile type, and 44px touch targets.
- Use placeholders honestly: temporary imagery can support the build, but launch requires real AATBS photos, approved stats, testimonials, and legal copy.
